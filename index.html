Who are we?

Hayley (videographer) will be at the wedding from the Bridal prep right through until after the first dance. If you throw a good party she might even stay longer. She loves a good atmosphere and loves nothing more than getting shots of them groovy moves that only come after a few too many drinks.

What do we do?

We will capture your special day in a fly on the wall style, ensuring all your special moments and reactions are captured in the most natural way possible, you will hardly even notice we are there. We then produce your video for you to watch back and remember all those precious little moments you probably forgot even happened.

Meet our team

Hayley

The creative one and the fly on the wall. Hayley is the one who will be at your even with her cameras catching your special day. The reason for our company name, she's scrawny! We were trying to come up with name, and as most of the family are ginger, we were coming up with all sorts of names with ginger in it… Ginger Ninja Media, Ginger Bread Man Films, Ginger Productions. The problem is Hayley is the only one who isn't ginger so it wouldn't really look right when she turned up! So what could we call it? She's very pale, but Ghostly Media sounded a bit creepy. Then BOOM!, it came to us! The charming nickname Ash has had for Hayley since they first met… Scrawndog, because, well… she's scrawny (although she's not a Dog). Perfect! Now it wont look so odd when a skinny female turns up instead of a Ginger man.

Ash

The boss! The one who you will speak to prior to the event, in charge of bookings and all the boring bits. Chief Editor, he spending his time with his head stuck in his computer, laughing at all the funny comments he pics up on the mics when people forget they are being recorded. Once he's had a laugh, he then produces a beautiful film for you to look back on for the rest of your lives together.

Freddy

Our son, the little Ginger gamer who is obsessed with Mario. He is currently adamant that when he grows up he wants to be Bowser… when he finally realises that he cant turn yellow and grow a green shell with spikes on, he might change his career path and maybe follow in our footsteps, he's definitely got the eye for it!

Jorgie

The baby of the family, couldn't care less about what we do and is more than happy as long as she's being fed!
